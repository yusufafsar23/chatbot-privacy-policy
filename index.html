Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

1. Introduction

ChatBot SaaS ("we", "our", or "us") operates an Instagram DM automation platform that helps e-commerce businesses respond to customer messages via the official Meta Messenger API. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and business details when you register.
  • Instagram / Facebook data: Instagram Business Account ID, Page ID, and page access tokens obtained through the Meta Login flow.
  • Message data: Incoming Instagram Direct Messages sent to your connected business account and the automated responses sent on your behalf via the Meta Messenger API (messaging_type: RESPONSE). We only reply to messages that users initiate.
  • Usage data: Log data and feature usage statistics for service improvement.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the chatbot automation service.
  • To send automated replies to Instagram DMs on your behalf using the official Meta Graph API.
  • To authenticate your Meta account and manage webhook subscriptions.
  • To display conversation analytics in your dashboard.
  • To send you service-related notifications and support communications.

4. Meta Platform Data

Our application integrates with the Meta Platform (Facebook / Instagram) and complies with the Meta Platform Terms and Meta Developer Policies.

  • We only request permissions necessary to send and receive messages (instagram_manage_messages, pages_messaging).
  • We do not sell, transfer, or use Meta user data for advertising or data brokers.
  • All messaging is strictly of type RESPONSE — we only reply to messages that users initiate.
  • We do not send unsolicited messages, spam, or promotional content.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with cloud hosting providers and AI providers (e.g. OpenAI) solely to operate the service, under strict data processing agreements. We may also disclose data if required by law.

6. Data Retention

Conversation data is retained for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.

7. Data Security

We implement TLS encryption in transit, encrypted storage at rest, and HMAC signature verification for all Meta webhook events. Access tokens are stored encrypted and scoped per business account.

8. Your Rights (GDPR / KVKK)

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data.
  • Data portability.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.
